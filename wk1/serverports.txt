What is the definition of server port?
	A server port is a communication end point. It is a way to identify a specific network processes and their messages when it 	arrives at the server.

What is server port range?
	Registered ports are in the rage of 1024 to 49151.
	Ports used by the system are from 1024 to 49151.
	Dynamic ports (used for personal use) are from 49152 to 65535.
